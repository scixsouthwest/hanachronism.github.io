  • Public Speaking Courses

    I run public speaking courses to increase participation in meetings within the Labour Party, rather than having the same people dominate the discussion. Mostly, I run courses specifically for women focussing on confidence and structure, but the same material can be used to give anyone the tools they need to ask questions, speak to motions and effectively join discussions.

    Please email hanachronism@gmail.com to discuss organising a course.
