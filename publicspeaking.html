  • Public Speaking Courses

    I run public speaking courses to increase participation in meetings within the Labour Party and trade unions. Mostly, I run courses specifically for women focussing on confidence and structure, but the same material can be used to give anyone the tools they need to ask questions, speak to motions and effectively join discussions.

    Please email hanachronism@gmail.com to discuss organising a course.
